The birthplace of the Renaissance

"Florence is a city in central Italy and the capital city of the Tuscany region.
It is the most-populated city in Tuscany.

Florence was a centre of medieval European trade and finance.
It was the birthplace of the Renaissance.

Forbes has ranked it as one of the most beautiful cities in the world
due to Florence's artistic and architectural heritage(Florence, 2020)."

Exploring Florence

Famous attractions in Florence

  1. Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore and Piazza Duomo

    Florence's specialty is the Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore, known as Duomo.
    It is 600 years old and still the largest brick and mortar dome in the world
    (Florence, 2020).

  2. Piazzale Michelangelo

    David, his representative sculptor, stands in bronze in honor of Renaissance
    sculptor Michelangelo(Florence, 2020).

  3. Ponte Vecchio

    This Bridge is a unique bridge with numerous shops.
    This is the only bridge in the city that was not broken during World War II
    (Florence, 2020).
